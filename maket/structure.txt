Website Structure Description

Header Section

Logo: Positioned top-left with the "SPACEX" logo.
Navigation Menu: Horizontal menu on the right side of the header with the following items:

Главная (Home)
Технологии (Technologies)
График полетов (Flight Schedule)
Гарантии (Guarantees)
О компании (About Us)
Контакты (Contacts)


Background: Dark space-themed background with stars.


Main Content Section

Background Image: A large image of Mars with a rocket launching towards it, centered vertically and horizontally.
Main Heading: Bold text "ПУТЕШЕСТВИЯ" (Journeys) in large white font, positioned slightly above the center.
Subheading: Smaller text "на красную планету" (to the red planet) below the main heading, in white.
Call-to-Action Button: A clickable button labeled "Начать путешествие" (Start Journey) below the subheading, styled with a modern border and hover effect.


Info Panels

Layout: Four rectangular panels arranged around the main image, two on the left and two on the right.
Left Panels:

Top Left:

Text: "мы" (we)
Subtext: "на рынке" (in the market)
Value: "1"


Bottom Left:

Text: "календари за" (calendars for)
Subtext: "в подарок" (as a gift)
Value: "2001"




Right Panels:

Top Right:

Text: "гарантируем" (we guarantee)
Subtext: "безопасность" (safety)
Value: "50%"


Bottom Right:

Text: "путешествие" (journey)
Subtext: "дней" (days)
Value: "597"






General Styling

Color Scheme: Dark background (#000000 or similar), white text (#FFFFFF), orange accents (#FF4500) for the Mars image and rocket.
Typography: Use a futuristic font (e.g., sans-serif like "Orbitron" or similar) for headings and values.
Responsiveness: Ensure the layout adjusts for mobile devices, stacking elements vertically if needed.
